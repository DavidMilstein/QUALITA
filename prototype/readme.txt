To see the prototype follow these steps:

-download justinmind: https://www.justinmind.com/
-download the file prototype1 (https://github.com/YonaMilstein/QUALITA/blob/master/prototype/Prototype1.vp)
-create an account in justinmind
-open the file prototype1
-download the app "justinmind" on your phone
-click on "view on device" in the site
-login with your account in the app
-click on "view on device" in the app
You will see our prototype :)
